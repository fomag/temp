Keep getting your payments

Working during an eligibility period

You may earn income while receiving the [ABC], but some restrictions apply to how much you can earn within an eligibility period. If you earn too much, you may need to return or repay the full payment ($[#]) for that period.

Having to repay or not will depend on if you continue to meet the eligibility criteria for that period. The timing for when you can earn income during your first eligibility period is different than during subsequent periods.

Eligibility ends after [#] periods

The [ABC] with the CRA terminates after you have received it for a maximum of [#] periods ([#] weeks). The periods do not have to be consecutive. After this time, you will not be eligible to receive the [ABC].
